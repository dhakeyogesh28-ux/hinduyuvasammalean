The Hindu Yuva Maha Sammelan is being organized on the occasion of Ram Navami on 26th March 2026 at Dongri Vasti Gruha, Gangapur Road, Nashik. More than 4,000 youth will gather to celebrate this special day with devotion and unity. The main highlight of the event will be the collective recitation of Hanuman Chalisa. Along with this, many cultural and inspirational programs will be conducted. The event aims to bring young people together, strengthen faith, and promote positive values in society.
